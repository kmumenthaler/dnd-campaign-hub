# DM screen

The DM screen is a sidebar reference panel with eight tabs covering the most commonly referenced D&D 5e rules. Open it from the sidebar or by running **Open DM Screen** from the Command Palette.

## Conditions

Shows D&D 5e conditions as clickable cards. If you have imported SRD condition data into `z_Conditions/`, the cards link to the corresponding vault notes. Otherwise the plugin displays 15 built-in conditions:

Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Grappled, Incapacitated, Invisible, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained, Stunned, Unconscious, and Exhaustion (with all six levels).

## Actions in combat

A categorized reference for what characters can do on their turn:

- **Actions** — Attack, Cast a Spell, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use an Object, Grapple, and Shove.
- **Bonus Actions** — Offhand Attack, certain class features, and certain spells.
- **Reactions** — Opportunity Attack, Ready action trigger, and certain spells.
- **Movement** — Stand Up, Crawl, High Jump, Long Jump, and Difficult Terrain.
- **Free Actions** — Drop item, communicate, and interact with one object.

## Combat

Quick-reference cards for core combat mechanics:

- Attack roll formula.
- Critical hits.
- Advantage and disadvantage.
- Death saving throws.
- Concentration.
- Initiative.
- Surprise.

## Skills

Skills grouped by ability score (Strength through Charisma) with the passive score formula.

## Travel and rest

- **Travel Pace table** — Fast, Normal, and Slow paces with distance per minute, hour, and day, plus effects.
- **Short Rest** and **Long Rest** rules.
- **Carrying Capacity** — regular and variant encumbrance rules.

## Difficulty classes

- **Typical DCs table** — values from 5 (Very Easy) through 30 (Nearly Impossible).
- **Spell Save DC** and **Spell Attack Modifier** formulas.
- **AC calculation** — no armor, light armor, medium armor, heavy armor, and shield.

## Damage

Shows D&D 5e damage types as reference cards. If you have imported SRD damage type data into `z_DamageTypes/`, the cards link to vault notes. Otherwise the plugin displays 13 built-in damage types with examples:

Acid, Bludgeoning, Cold, Fire, Force, Lightning, Necrotic, Piercing, Poison, Psychic, Radiant, Slashing, and Thunder.

Rules for resistance, vulnerability, and immunity are included.

## Cover

- **Cover table** — Half Cover (+2 AC/DEX saves), Three-Quarters Cover (+5 AC/DEX saves), and Total Cover.
- **Obscurement** — Lightly and Heavily Obscured areas.
- **Light** — Bright Light, Dim Light, and Darkness.
- **Special Vision** — Darkvision, Blindsight, Truesight, and Tremorsense.
